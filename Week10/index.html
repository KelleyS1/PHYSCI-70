First error & solution

1)Following the first tutorial,I installed the boards package into the preferences and then downloaded the "esp32" board form the board manager.
2) Now that the drivers and boards are installed, I opened the "SimpleWifiServer" from the examples within Arduino IDE. 3) Inputted my SSID and corresponding password into the boilerplate examples and uploaded. The compile went smoothly. Actually the smoothest compile I've had in weeks!
The PROBLEM
4) Opened the Serial Monitor and got a bunch of "?????"

The FIX
Luckily this one is pretty easy. Within the serial monitor, change the baud rate to 115200 and fixed . Usually



Second error & solution

1) Now text appears instead of the "???" and it says it accessing my network
2) A "....." appears. Wait
The PROBLEM
3) The Serial monitor doesn't progress further and instead continues to spit out "..." as it fails to connect.

The FIX
For me, this problem was not as obvious. After some googling and reading, I fixed out that I had put in the 5 gHz connection and that is the issue. Once I entered in the 2.4 gHz connection, reuploaded, and hit the reset button. Things progressed. Fixed.
