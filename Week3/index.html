Week 3: Hands tools and Fabrication


This week, I'm not entirely sure I fulfilled the prompt, unfortunately, but I did make something that moves! I was focused on a moving kinect piece rather than one of joints. But I did use a ton of tools and worked with many different materials, such as brass, acyrillic, wood. And I used many of the tools in the lab to cut these materials. Which I think was the heart of this week...using the hand tools and making something new.
